Designer.

I wear a lot of different hats. I'm a student, a husband, an artist, a front-end developer, a Mormon, and an ISFP. I have been doing freelance illustration and graphic design for 9 years, and web design and development for 2 years. I haven't found a niche yet, and don't really want to. "Cool as a cucumber" is not how I would describe my internal dialogue.

Digital Painting.

First and foremost, I'm an illustrator! It's my life's passion, and has influenced every facet of my life and design philosophy. I've done a wide variety of work, ranging from advertising to concept art, and from one screen printed tee shirts to 40 foot long murals. "Style" is overrated, and I've cultivated quite a few stylistic arrows in my quiver. Check out my Illustration portfolio for a few samples of my past work.
